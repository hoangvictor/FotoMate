About us

Fotomate is an online platform that uses a web/mobile application to match customers and nearest photographers. Fotomate gives you access to the following features:

  • GPS helps you quickly find the nearest photographer.
  • The variety of choices according to your needs.
  • Intelligent search and selection make it easy to find the right photographer.

Each photo gives us unforgettable memories. However, most images don't have our whole family members, and the reason is that we choose to take pictures by ourselves. Not only that, when we want to have a professional photographer, we lose too much time searching and comparing prices between photographers, ... These issues prompted us to create a booking platform from local photographers. With reasonable prices, wholehearted payment support and user protection policy, you can enjoy your life pleasantly without worrying about missing precious memories.
